Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 225g spaghetti
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4-6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes to the oil. Cook for about 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently, until the garlic turns golden and aromatic.
  4. Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can become bitter.
  5. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it well, and add it to the skillet with the garlic and oil.
  6. Toss the pasta gently in the garlic oil, ensuring that the strands are well coated.
  7. Gradually add some of the reserved pasta water to the skillet, a couple of tablespoons at a time, while tossing the pasta.
  8. Continue adding the water until the desired consistency is achieved.
  9. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the pasta and season with salt to taste.
  10. Toss everything together once more to distribute the flavors evenly.
  11. Serve the Spaghetti Aglio e Olio with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top, if desired.
