Caprese Salad

Description

Discover the taste of summer with our vibrant and refreshing Caprese salad recipe. Bursting with the freshest flavors, this Italian classic showcases the harmonious blend of ripe tomatoes, creamy fresh mozzarella, fragrant basil leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze and olive oil. Each bite transports you to sun-kissed Mediterranean shores, where simplicity and quality reign supreme. Elevate your salad game with this easy-to-make yet elegant dish that celebrates the essence of seasonal produce.

Perfect as an appetizer, light lunch, or a delightful side, our Caprese salad is a delightful ode to the art of simplicity, leaving your taste buds longing for more. Enjoy a burst of sunshine on your plate and savor the authentic taste of Italy with every forkful. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 225g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. On a serving platter or individual plates, arrange the sliced tomatoes in a single layer, slightly overlapping them for a visually appealing presentation.
  2. Place a slice of fresh mozzarella on top of each tomato slice.
  3. Take fresh basil leaves and gently tuck them in between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. Drizzle balsamic glaze and extra-virgin olive oil over the tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  5. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper over the salad, to taste.
  6. Your Caprese Salad is ready to be served! Enjoy it as a light and refreshing appetizer or a delightful side dish, perfect for any occasion.
